Goat Confit

Description

Goat meat is usually very lean and need long time in the oven on low temperature to become really tender.

This recipe is really easy since the meat cooks itself in the oven and makes a really delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 goat legs
  • 300 g salt
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 1 pot rosemarine
  • 2 bay leafs
  • 1 ts pepper
  • 2 L corn oil
  • 1kg Jerusalem artichoke
  • 2 L water
  • 1 dl milk
  • 2 ts butter

Steps

  1. Rub the meat in salt and put the it in the fridge for the night.
  2. Wash the salt away and dry the meat. Rub it with cutted garlic, crushed bayleaf, rosemarine and pepper. Leave it in room temperature for 1-2 hour.
  3. Put the meat in an ovenproof dish, put the oven on 120 Â°C for 3 hours. Take it out and let it cool.
  4. Boil the water and let the artichoke cook until they are tender.
  5. Remove the water and add butter and milk.
  6. Mash it until you get the consistency you want
  7. Serve the goat with the mashed artichoke
