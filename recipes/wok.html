Wok

Description

Delicious wok recipe with a lot of good seasonflavours

Ingredients

  • 800 g noodles
  • 2 ts soy sauce
  • 2 ts oyster sauce
  • 2 ts brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons oil
  • 500 g shrimp
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1/4 yellow only, thinly sliced
  • 3 crimini mushrooms sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Cook noodles in a pot with boiling water.
  2. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, fish sauce, garlic powder, ginger in a bowl until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Heat oil in large wok, cook and stir shrimp in hot oil. Add the vegetables and fry together. Add noodles and sauce and mix it for 5 minutes.
