Pizza

Description

Quick and easy pizza with tomato sauce pepperoni and pizza.

Ingredients

Pizza sauce

  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 ts oregano
  • 1 ts dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Pizza crust

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 bags of Pizza Crust yeast
  • 1 ts sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon
  • 1 1/3 cups warm vater
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • Pepperoni
  • 1 cup cheese

Steps

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a bowl, set aside
  2. Combine 2 cups of flour with yeast, sugar and salt. Add water and oil and mix until blended. Add the remaining flour slowly until you have a nice dough.
  3. Knead until dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Pat the dough into a pizza pan.
  5. Preheat oven to 225C. Top crusts with sauce pepperoni and cheese.
  6. Bake for 12-14 minutes.
